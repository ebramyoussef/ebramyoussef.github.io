About Me

Ebram Youssef is an undergraduate student (senior) at the University of Rochester Pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Physics with minors in Computer Science and Math, expected to graduate in May 2023. Ebram currently works as a research assistant at Boyd Quantum Photonics Research Group . Ebram's areas of interest include: Nonlinear optics, quantum photonics, quantum mechanics, particle physics, condensed-matter physics, computational physics, and machine learning.
